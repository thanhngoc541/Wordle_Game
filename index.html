Wordle Game

POINT SYSTEM

Points are awarded based on the number of word attempts used to solve the Wordle puzzles and your speed.


Attempts

1 - 250 points

2 - 200 points

3 - 150 points

4 - 100 points

5 - 50 points

6 - 25 points


Speed

0:00-0:29 seconds - 500 points

0:30-0:59 seconds - 450 points

1:00-1:29 minutes - 400 points

1:30-1:59 minutes - 350 points

2:00-2:29 minutes - 300 points

2:30-2:59 minutes - 250 points

3:00-3:29 minutes - 200 points

3:30-3:59 minutes - 150 points

4:00-4:29 minutes - 100 points

4:30-4:59 minutes - 50 points

5:00+ minutes - 10 points

INSTRUCTIONS


  1. You have six attempts to answer the correct five letter word.

  2. Type in your guess and submit the word by pressing enter.

  3. The letter tiles will change colours once you have submitted your word. 

  • Yellow = correct letter in the wrong spot

  • Green = correct letter in the correct spot

  • Grey = letter not included in the word

  1. Use your attempts to solve the word before you run out of guesses. 


Have fun and good luck!
